How to Change Title and Meta Description in Magento 2

Published on: 28/8/2024

Author: Shivbhadrasinh Gohil

Google and other search engines keep changing their algorithms, which leads SEO professionals to adapt to new SEO strategies. However, titles and meta descriptions are still crucial from an SEO perspective.

Proper titles and descriptions for your Magento 2 website pages can help your store secure better SEO rankings and boost visibility. In other words, they can help you get more customers to your store.

In this guide, you will learn how to change the title and meta description in Magento 2.

Magento 2 Title & Meta Description: What Are They & When to Edit

Before you learn how to change titles and meta descriptions in Magento 2 website pages, let’s walk you through what they are –

What is a Title?

The title of a website page is the main text that describes what the page is about. It appears in search results at the top of the meta description. A well-written title can help you draw your customers’ attention.

What is a Meta Description?

A meta description provides a summary of your web page’s content. It appears below the title in search engine results. A good meta description tells your customers what they can expect on the website page.

Now, if you are wondering when to change titles and meta descriptions. Here are some reasons to look for:

  • Your pages are performing poorly from the SEO perspective.
  • Your title and meta description do not describe your web page’s content.
  • Your CTR from search engines is low

How to Change Meta Title in Magento 2?

Let’s walk you through how to change the meta title in Magento 2:

How to Edit Title in Magento 2?

Step 1: Head to Content > Design > Configuration

  • Login to Magento 2 and go to the dashboard.
  • From the left sidebar, click on Content > Design and select Configuration.

Step 2: Navigate to HTML > Default Page Title

  • In the Configuration section, choose the store view you want to edit.
  • Click Edit on the right side of the store view.
  • Scroll down to the HTML Head section.
  • Find the Default Page Title and enter your new meta title. After entering the new title, click Save Configuration at the top right.

How to Edit Meta Description in Magento 2?

Although the initial steps are the same in adding the meta description in Magento 2, let’s walk you through from the beginning again –

Step 1: Head to Content > Design > Configuration

  • Again, go to the Magento 2 dashboard.
  • Click on Content > Design, and select Configuration.

Step 2: Navigate to HTML > Default Meta Description

  • Choose the store view you want to edit and click Edit.
  • Scroll down to the HTML Head section.
  • Find the Default Meta Description and enter your new meta description.
  • After entering the new description, click Save Configuration at the top right.

Tips for Writing SEO-friendly Title & Meta Description

Writing SEO-friendly titles and meta descriptions is crucial for the overall performance of your website’s SEO ranking. You can follow the below tips to write high-performing titles and meta descriptions –

  • Keep your titles and meta descriptions concise and clear. Also, ensure they are within the recommended length (title: 50-60 characters, description: 150-160 characters). You can use Meetanshi’s free tool to check the character length.
  • Don’t miss out on the opportunities to incorporate primary and secondary keywords. However, don’t use keywords forcefully.
  • Provide a compelling CTA to inspire your customers to click on your website’s link.
  • Don’t plagiarize titles and meta descriptions. Keep them unique.
  • If possible, include your brand’s name.
