Published on: 28/8/2024

Author: Shivbhadrasinh Gohil

Managing collections in your Shopify store can be overwhelming without using the right tools.

Choosing the best collection manager app helps to streamline and align operations to boost the overall store performance.

In this blog post know the 3 best Shopify collection manager apps available in the Shopify app store and pick the one that best fits your needs.

Best Shopify Collection Manager Apps In 2024

1. Meet Collections Import Export

If you are looking to migrate collections from one Shopify store to another, the Meet Collections Import Export is your go-to app. It helps you quickly import and export collection data in bulk through CSV files.

Key Features:

  • Easily export automated collections with selected conditions
  • Export your manual collections with or without assigned product data
  • Keeps track of all collection exports and imports history

Pricing:

  • Free plan available
  • Basic: $3.99/month

The Meet Collection Import Export app eliminates the need to migrate collections manually.

As you migrate products and other entities, Shopify does not have a default way to import or export collections. With this app, you can migrate Shopify collections in seconds with just a few navigations

2. ST: Product and Collection Sort

It is a comprehensive merchandising app that helps to design, automate, and optimize product sorting on each Shopify collection page. Utilizing advanced data analytics and historical order data, the app intelligently sorts products on criteria and helps reduce manual efforts.

Key Features:

  • Accurate and advanced data analytics that helps in utilizing past order data and analytics to easily sort products effectively.
  • Provides custom sorting criteria such as sort by views, add-to-cart, revenue, inventory management, new launches, and other availability.
  • Helps with flexible scheduling and configuring products that help in sorting to run on-demand products.

Pricing:

  • Free plan available
  • Basic: $19/month
  • Business: $49/month
  • Unlimited: $179/month

3. Entaice: AI Collection Sort

Entaice is an automated merchandising app that is designed to boost Shopify store performance by continuously optimizing products and sorting on collection pages.

It helps to leverage the AI by dynamically adjusting product rankings based on inventory levels and availability. It ensures that out-of-stock items are pushed down to the bottom while keeping the trending posts on the top and promoted. It drives sales efficiently and faster.

Key Features:

  • AI-powered sorting helps automatically sort the overall inventory merchandise collections every 2 hours.
  • Out-of-stock products to show at the last and most-rated products to showcase on the top that helps in increasing sales.
  • Dynamic adjustment helps in product ranking according to inventory and availability

Pricing:

  • Free to install
