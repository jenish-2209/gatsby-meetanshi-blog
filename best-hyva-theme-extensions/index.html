What are Magento 2 Hyvä Theme Extensions? 

Magento 2 Hyvä theme extensions are add-ons designed to enhance the capabilities and performance of Magento stores using the Hyvä theme. 

These extensions allow store owners to customize their websites without changing the core code, making it easier to add new features and improve user experience.

Key Features of Hyvä Theme Extensions:

  • They help to optimize site speed and responsiveness, providing a better shopping experience for customers.
  • Hyvä theme extensions are created to work well with the Hyvä framework, making installation and updates easy.
  • These extensions can improve navigation, product displays, and overall usability, keeping customers engaged and encouraging them to make purchases.
  • Many extensions offer tracking and reporting features, allowing store owners to analyze customer behavior and sales trends for better decision-making.

By using Magento 2 Hyvä compatible extensions, store owners can create a more dynamic, efficient, and user-friendly online shopping experience.

Magento 2 Hyvä Compatible Extensions: Comparison 

5 Best Hyvä Theme Extensions for 2024

1. Magento 2 B2B Registration Form

Wholesalers are increasingly favoring eCommerce, and the Magento 2 B2B registration form extension is perfect for creating a segmentation between B2B and B2C sign-up. 

This extension simplifies the process of managing B2B customer registrations, offering a dedicated sign-up form and allowing you to approve/reject registration forms from the backend.

Key Features: 

  • A separate registration form with additional fields for company name, business type, and VAT number.
  • Approve or reject B2B customer registrations to ensure thorough verification.
  • Automatic alerts for you and your customers regarding registration status.
  • Add Google reCAPTCHA v3 to prevent spam registrations.
  • Assign approved customers to specific groups for better management.

Pricing:

  • $ 49 + Free 1 Year Updates & Supports

2. Magento 2 Shipping Restrictions

As a store owner, you put a lot of effort into your products and marketing, but managing shipping can be challenging. Here’s how the Magento 2 shipping restrictions extension can prove to be a lifesaver. 

This extension lets you limit shipping methods based on cart details, customer groups, and store views. You can customize your shipping policy to ensure every shipment meets your business needs.

Key Features: 

  • Streamlines your shipping processes to save time and reduce costs.
  • Ensures that your shipping methods meet local legal requirements.
  • Set shipping limits based on: Cart attributes, Customer attributes, Customer groups, Store views, and more.
  • Displays multiple error messages for shipping restrictions directly on the cart page if applicable.
  • Apply shipping restrictions to orders created from the backend.

Pricing: 

  • $99.00

3. Magento 2 PayPal Website Payments Pro- H3

The Magento 2 PayPal Website Payments Pro extension helps you accept payments easily and securely. This extension allows customers to pay using their credit or debit cards without needing a PayPal account. 

When customers check out, they can simply enter their card details directly on your website, keeping everything smooth and hassle-free. 

This way, they can complete their purchases without being redirected elsewhere, ensuring a better shopping experience in your Magento 2 store.

Key Features: 

  • Set a unique title for the payment method.
  • Display the payment method logo at checkout.
  • Accept specific credit card types and limit payment to selected countries.
  • Admins can view payment details and place orders from the backend.

Pricing: 

  • Request for pricing

4. Magento 2 Delivery Date

Make your Magento 2 store more customer-friendly with the Delivery Date extension. This extension lets customers pick their preferred delivery date and time during checkout. 

By allowing them to choose when they want their orders, you can make shopping easier and improve customer satisfaction.

Key Features:

  • Customers can select a preferred delivery date and time slot during checkout.
  • Option for customers to leave messages for the delivery person.
  • Set available delivery dates and times, block holidays or specific days, and configure the calendar settings.
  • View delivery date and time details in the admin panel, including in order, invoice, and shipment views.

Pricing: 

  • $69.00

5. Magento 2 Zoho CRM Integration

The Magento 2 Zoho CRM Integration extension allows store owners to connect their Magento 2 store with Zoho CRM for efficient data management. 

This powerful extension automates routine tasks, streamlines daily operations, and ensures seamless synchronization between both platforms, all without the need for technical expertise.

Key Features: 

  • Choose to sync data right away or set it up to sync automatically at scheduled times.
  • Sync important data like campaigns, accounts, contacts, leads, products, orders, and invoices.
  • Automate how you manage leads and contacts, making your workflows smoother.
  • Sync pricing rules to keep track of your marketing campaigns effectively.
  • Check the status of your data sync with an easy-to-understand report.

Pricing: 

  • $299
